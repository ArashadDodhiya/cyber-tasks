# 🧠 30-Day Hacker Mindset Challenge

## What Is This?

This is a **30-day structured challenge** designed to rewire your thinking from a regular user/developer into someone who sees systems the way hackers do — by questioning everything, finding what others miss, and thinking creatively about how things break.

## How It Works

| Week | Theme | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Week 1** | 👁️ Observe | Learn to see attack surfaces everywhere |
| **Week 2** | ❓ Question | Develop the "but what if..." instinct |
| **Week 3** | 💥 Exploit | Think creatively about breaking things |
| **Week 4** | 🎯 Master | Apply it all in real-world scenarios |

## Rules

1. **One task per day** — Each day has a focused exercise (~30-60 minutes)
2. **Document everything** — Write down your findings in a journal/notes
3. **No skipping** — Even if a task seems easy, do it. Repetition builds instinct
4. **Legal only** — Only practice on targets you own or have permission to test (labs, CTFs, your own apps)
5. **Reflect daily** — After each task, write one sentence: *"Today I learned that..."*
