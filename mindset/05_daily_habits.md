# ✅ Daily Hacker Habits Checklist

> **Use this daily alongside the 30-day challenge. These micro-habits compound over time.**

---

## 🌅 Morning Routine (10 min)

- [ ] **Read one security article** — Pick from:
  - [The Hacker News](https://thehackernews.com/)
  - [Krebs on Security](https://krebsonsecurity.com/)
  - [BleepingComputer](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/)
  - [Dark Reading](https://www.darkreading.com/)
- [ ] **Review one CVE** — Check [CVE.org](https://www.cve.org/) or [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/) for a recent vulnerability

---

## 💻 During Screen Time (Ongoing)

- [ ] **Inspect one website** — View source, check headers, read `robots.txt`
- [ ] **Question one feature** — "How could this be abused?"
- [ ] **Spot one assumption** — "What is the developer trusting that they shouldn't?"

---

## 🔧 Practice Session (30-60 min)

- [ ] **Hands-on time** — Work on your current day's challenge
- [ ] **Use one tool** — Practice with Burp Suite, Nmap, SQLMap, etc.
- [ ] **Write one script/payload** — Even a small one

---

## 🌙 Evening Reflection (5 min)

- [ ] **Today I learned that...**
  > *(Write one sentence about your key learning)*

- [ ] **Tomorrow I want to explore...**
  > *(Write what grabbed your curiosity today)*

- [ ] **One thing that surprised me...**
  > *(Unexpected behavior, finding, or concept)*
