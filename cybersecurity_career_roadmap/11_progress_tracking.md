## 📊 Skill Level Definitions

Use this rubric for honest self-assessment:

| Score | Level | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 1-2 | Aware | You know the topic exists and can define it |
| 3-4 | Beginner | You can follow tutorials and solve guided labs |
| 5-6 | Intermediate | You can solve problems independently with occasional reference |
| 7-8 | Proficient | You can teach others and solve novel problems |
| 9-10 | Expert | You can find new vulnerabilities and build tools in this area |

---

## 🔄 How to Use This Tracking System

1. **Every Sunday:** Fill out the Weekly Scorecard (10 minutes)
2. **Every month (1st):** Complete the Monthly Self-Assessment (30 minutes)
3. **Every month:** Check off completed milestones in the Master Checklist
4. **Every quarter (Month 3, 6, 9, 12):** Write a retrospective (1 hour)
   - What worked?
   - What didn't work?
   - What will I change?
   - Am I on track for my goals?
5. **If a score stays flat for 2+ months:** Something is wrong. Either you're not practicing enough, or you need to change your approach.

## 🚨 Warning Signs You're Off Track

| Warning Sign | What It Means | Action |
|-------------|--------------|--------|
| Haven't opened a terminal in 3+ days | You're in theory mode | Drop everything, open a lab |
| 0 blog posts in a month | Documentation debt growing | Write 1 post this week, even a short one |
| No new connections in 2 weeks | Networking is dying | Spend 30 min on LinkedIn today |
| Overall energy below 4/10 for 2+ weeks | Burnout approaching | Take a full day off, reduce schedule |
| Same skill score for 3 months | You've plateaued | Change your practice method or topic |
| 0 job applications in Month 6+ | Fear is winning | Apply to 5 jobs TODAY, quality doesn't matter |
| Skipping exercise for 2+ weeks | Physical health declining | Start with 10-minute walks, rebuild |
| Comparing yourself to others daily | Mental health risk | Unfollow triggering accounts, focus on YOUR scorecard |
