# 📜 Certifications Roadmap

> **"Certifications don't make you a hacker. But they open doors, validate skills, and give structure to your learning."**

---

## 🎯 Strategy

Certifications should be pursued AS you build skills, not instead of building skills. Use them as milestones and validation checkpoints.

---

## 📊 Certification Tiers

### Tier 1: Foundation (Year 1)

| Certification | Provider | Focus | Cost | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **eJPT** | INE Security | Junior Pentester | ~$250 | ⭐⭐ |
| **PNPT** | TCM Security | Network Pentesting | ~$400 | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **CEH Practical** | EC-Council | Ethical Hacking | ~$550 | ⭐⭐ |

**Recommended:** Start with **eJPT** or **PNPT** — both are practical and affordable.

### Tier 2: Professional (Year 1-2)

| Certification | Provider | Focus | Cost | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **OSCP** | OffSec | Penetration Testing | ~$1,600 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **BSCP** | PortSwigger | Web Security | ~$100 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **CRTP** | Altered Security | AD Pentesting | ~$300 | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **CPTS** | HackTheBox | Penetration Testing | ~$500 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

**Recommended:** **OSCP** is the gold standard. **BSCP** is excellent for web specialists. **CRTP** for AD focus.

### Tier 3: Advanced (Year 2-3)

| Certification | Provider | Focus | Cost | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **OSWE** | OffSec | White-Box Web | ~$1,600 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **OSEP** | OffSec | Evasion & Red Team | ~$1,600 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **CRTO** | Zero-Point Security | Red Team Ops | ~$500 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **CRTE** | Altered Security | AD Expert | ~$350 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **OSED** | OffSec | Exploit Development | ~$1,600 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

**Recommended:** **OSEP** for red team, **OSWE** for web, **CRTO** for accessible red team cert.

### Tier 4: Expert (Year 3+)

| Certification | Provider | Focus | Cost | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **OSCE3** | OffSec | OSWE + OSEP + OSED combined | ~$5,000+ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **GXPN** | SANS/GIAC | Exploit Researcher | ~$8,000+ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **GREM** | SANS/GIAC | Reverse Engineering | ~$8,000+ | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

## 🗓️ Suggested Certification Timeline

```
Month 3-4:   eJPT or PNPT ← validate foundations
Month 8-10:  BSCP ← validate web mastery
Month 11-14: OSCP ← the industry standard
Month 15-18: CRTP/CRTE ← validate AD skills
Month 20-24: CRTO ← validate red team skills
Month 24-30: OSEP or OSWE ← advanced specialization
Month 30+:   OSED / OSCE3 ← elite tier
```

---

## 💡 Certification Tips

1. **Don't cert-stack without skills** — a cert without practical ability is worthless
2. **Use free labs first** — PortSwigger, TryHackMe, HackTheBox before paying for cert labs
3. **Budget wisely** — start with affordable certs (eJPT, BSCP, CRTP, CRTO)
4. **OSCP is king for job applications** — prioritize it if seeking employment
5. **Document your prep** — write notes and create study guides (you'll reference them forever)
6. **Practice under exam conditions** — time yourself, simulate exam stress
